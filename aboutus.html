AGHicks Building Services and Property Maintenance has been operating in and around Northampton for the past 20 years and aim to provide the most efficient, friendly and reliable service in the local area.

We offer a full range of domestic building services, including home extensions, conservatories, plastering, renovations, kitchen and bathroom installations, window and door refits, tiling, fencing, bricklaying, driveways, fascias, garages and carports, carpentry, patios, guttering and stonework.

Our main areas of coverage include Northampton, Kettering, Corby, Wellingborough, Olney, Irchester, Finedon, Irthlingborough, Sywell, Thrapston and Yardley Hastings. Whilst we generally operate within a 20 mile radius of Northampton, we can make exceptions if necessary.

Regardless of the size of the job, we are proud to say that we offer FREE quotes to all customers.
